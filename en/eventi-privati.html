Birthdays da ricordare

A big table, a beautiful place, loved ones. It sounds simple — but finding it in Rome's historic centre, without breaking the bank, is not so straightforward.

At Suburra 1930 we organise birthdays for 10 to 60 guests. We take care of everything: custom menu, cake, decorations if you like, welcome cocktail.

Private room available on request
Dedicated menu with choice of starter, first course, main and dessert
Cocktails di benvenuto incluso su prenotazione di gruppo
Personalised cake — we coordinate with external pastry shops
Option to personalise table decorations

Graduation parties

Years of study, one day to celebrate. You need a place that lives up to the occasion — beautiful, central, with food that doesn't disappoint and service you won't regret.

We organise graduation parties all year round. The Sala Leonina, on the upper floor, is perfect for groups of 15–25 who want a more intimate and private evening.

Private Sala Leonina for 15–25 guests
Aperitivo + dinner package with set menu
Cocktails di benvenuto al bancone del bar
Option to bring personalised cake and gifts
Nearby parking — we recommend Via Labicana car park

Corporate dinners

A dinner with clients, a team lunch, an end-of-year event. The venue is part of the message — and a square from 100 AD steps from the Colosseum says something very specific.

We offer flexible solutions: from a fully private room to a dedicated table in the main dining room, with set menu or à la carte, with or without personalised catering service.

Private room for up to 60 people
Corporate set menu with 3 or 4 courses
Wine and spirits selection on request
